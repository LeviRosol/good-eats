## Overview
Hard‑browned beef spaghetti with basil‑butter finish and optional pesto swirl, plus roasted garlic bread with crunchy panko.
- Serves: 4
- Gear: 12‑in skillet or Dutch oven, pasta pot, sheet pan, foil, small skillet, broiler

## Shopping snapshot
- Spaghetti (12–16 oz)
- Ground beef (1–1.25 lb)
- Jarred marinara (24–32 oz)
- Tomato paste (small can)
- Red wine (small splash; optional)
- Onion (1 small; optional)
- Fresh basil (handful)
- Butter (stick)
- Parmesan
- Olive oil
- Garlic heads (1–2)
- Small French bread loaf
- Panko (for crunch)
- Kosher salt & black pepper
- Pepper Pesto (on hand)

## Timeline (~30 minutes)
0:00 – 0:05 Roast garlic
- Wrap garlic heads with olive oil + salt; into 400°F oven (35–45 min).

0:05 – 0:15 Brown beef, start sauce
- Boil pasta water. Hard‑sear beef; toast tomato paste; deglaze with wine; add jar sauce and simmer.

0:15 – 0:20 Toast panko
- Golden in a small skillet with oil/butter + pinch salt.

0:18 – 0:23 Cook pasta
- To al dente; reserve a mug of pasta water.

0:23 – 0:27 Finish sauce and bread
- Mash roasted garlic with softened butter. Stir butter + basil into sauce; season.
- Lightly toast bread under broiler; spread garlic butter; broil to golden.

0:27 – 0:30 Plate
- Toss pasta with sauce + splash of pasta water. Optional pesto swirl in bowls. Top with Parm, basil, and crunchy panko. Serve with garlic bread.
